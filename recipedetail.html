Recipe: Pancakes

Estimated Calorie Intake: 350 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Provides energy for the day
  • Contains fiber and protein
  • Rich in vitamins and minerals

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter (for frying)

Instructions

  • Mix all the dry ingredients together.
  • Add the wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  • Heat a pan and cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  • Serve with maple syrup.
